```

Hi [Retailer Name] Team,

notification service for South African shoe shoppers.

What we do:
- Users request specific shoes (brand, size, color)
- We monitor SA retailers including yours
- We notify users when their shoe becomes available
- Users click through to purchase on your site

Why partner with us:
- Targeted traffic: Users click when ready to buy
- Increased conversions: They're looking for specific products
- Professional site with legal compliance
- Privacy-first approach (POPIA compliant)

I'd love to discuss an affiliate partnership or explore other ways
we could work together.

Website: [Your URL]
About Us: [Link to about.html]

Looking forward to hearing from you!

Best regards,
Arno Kotze
findmysizealerts@gmail.com
```
