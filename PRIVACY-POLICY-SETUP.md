## 📝 What You Need To Customize

Open `privacy-policy.html` and search for these placeholders:

### 1. Information Officer Details (Section 14)
**Find this section:**
```html
<p style="margin-top: 15px;">
    <strong>FindMySize</strong><br>
    Information Officer: [YOUR NAME]<br>
    Email: <a href="mailto:privacy@findmysize.co.za">privacy@findmysize.co.za</a><br>
    <!-- Add physical address when you have one -->
    <!-- Address: [Your Physical Address] -->
</p>
```

**Replace with:**
- `[YOUR NAME]` - Your actual name
- `privacy@findmysize.co.za` - Your actual email address (can be your regular email)
- Optionally add your physical address if you have a business address

### 2. Update Email Addresses
