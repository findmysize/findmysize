What We Do

FindMySize is a free notification service for shoe shoppers in South Africa. We monitor popular online retailers and send you an email the moment your exact shoe size becomes available or goes on sale.

How It Works:

  1. You tell us what you want - Brand, model, size, color, width - as specific or flexible as you like
  2. We watch the stores - We monitor Takealot, Superbalist, Sportscene, and other South African retailers
  3. You get notified instantly - When your shoe becomes available, we email you with a direct link
  4. You decide what to do - Buy it, compare prices, or keep waiting - totally up to you

No more daily checking. No more missed restocks. No more disappointment.

How We Make Money

You might be wondering: "If it's free, how do you make money?"

Fair question! Here's our honest answer:

We participate in affiliate marketing programs with South African retailers. When we send you a notification, the link may be an affiliate link. If you decide to purchase through that link, the retailer pays us a small commission (typically 3-8% of the sale).

Important Things You Should Know:

  • You pay the same price - Affiliate commissions don't increase what you pay. The retailer pays us, not you
  • We don't change our recommendations - We notify you based ONLY on what YOU requested, not which retailer pays better commissions
  • Full transparency - We're upfront about this in our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • You're in control - You can shop wherever you want. Our links are just convenient shortcuts

This model lets us keep the service free for you while covering our costs for monitoring retailers and sending notifications.

What We're NOT

To set clear expectations:

  • We are NOT a shoe store - We don't sell shoes, we just help you find them
  • We are NOT affiliated with retailers - We're independent and monitor all stores equally
  • We DON'T guarantee availability - Stock changes constantly, and popular sizes sell fast
  • We DON'T control pricing - Retailers set their own prices
  • We DON'T handle returns or customer service - That's between you and the retailer

We're here to save you time and help you find your shoes. The rest is up to you and the retailers.
