Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

In Simple Terms: We collect your email and shoe preferences to notify you when shoes you want become available. We don't sell your data, we don't spam you, and you can unsubscribe anytime. We comply with South African data protection laws (POPIA).

1. Introduction

Welcome to FindMySize ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

This policy complies with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013, South Africa's data protection legislation.

By using FindMySize, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use our service, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address - To send you notifications about shoe availability
  • Shoe Preferences - Including brand, model, size, width, color, gender category, and style codes
  • Product URLs - Links you provide to help us identify specific shoes
  • Timestamp - When you submitted your request

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

We do not currently collect cookies, tracking data, or device information. However, basic server logs may record your IP address and browser type for security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Primary Service - To notify you when shoes matching your preferences become available at South African retailers
  • Service Improvement - To understand which shoes and brands are most requested
  • Communication - To send you alerts about shoes you've requested
  • Legal Compliance - To comply with applicable laws and regulations

What We DON'T Do:

  • We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do NOT share your email with retailers or advertisers
  • We do NOT send marketing emails unrelated to your shoe requests
  • We do NOT use your data for any purpose other than providing our service

4. Affiliate Disclosure

FindMySize participates in affiliate marketing programs. When we notify you about shoe availability, we may include affiliate links to retailer websites. If you make a purchase through these links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

This helps us keep the service free and running. The commission does not influence which shoes we notify you about - we notify you based solely on your requested preferences and actual product availability.

Retailers we work with may include: Takealot, Superbalist, Sportscene, Totalsports, The Athletes Foot, and other South African shoe retailers.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

  • Google Sheets - We store your request data in Google Sheets (secured by Google's infrastructure)
  • Email Service - We use Gmail/Google Workspace to send notification emails

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

5.3 Business Transfer

If FindMySize is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owners, who will continue to honor this privacy policy.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

6.2 Right to Correction

You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.

6.3 Right to Deletion

You can request that we delete your personal information. We will comply unless we have a legal obligation to retain it.

6.4 Right to Object

You can object to our processing of your personal information for certain purposes.

6.5 Right to Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe from our notifications at any time by replying to any email with "UNSUBSCRIBE" or contacting us directly.

To Exercise Your Rights: Contact us using the details at the bottom of this page. We will respond within 30 days as required by POPIA.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure:

  • Data is stored in Google Sheets with restricted access
  • We use HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Active Requests - Until you unsubscribe or the request is fulfilled
  • Notified Requests - We may keep records for up to 12 months for analytics purposes
  • Deletion Requests - We will delete your data within 30 days of your request

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete it immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Google's servers, which may be located outside South Africa. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place as required by POPIA.

11. Third-Party Links

Our notifications contain links to third-party retailer websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before making purchases.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Sending an email notification to registered users (for major changes)

Your continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Consent

By submitting your information through our form, you consent to:

  • The collection and processing of your personal information as described in this policy
  • Receiving email notifications about shoe availability
  • The use of affiliate links in our notifications

You can withdraw your consent at any time by unsubscribing or contacting us.
