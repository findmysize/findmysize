🚀 Getting Started

What is FindMySize? ›

FindMySize is a free notification service that helps you find shoes in your size. Instead of manually checking multiple retailer websites every day, you tell us what shoe you want, and we monitor South African retailers for you. When your requested shoe becomes available or goes on sale, we email you with a direct link.

It's like having a personal shopping assistant watching the stores 24/7 so you never miss a restock again!

How much does it cost? ›

FindMySize is 100% free.

No subscription fees, no hidden costs, no credit card required. Just sign up and get notified. We make money through affiliate commissions from retailers (see "How We Make Money" below), but this doesn't cost you anything extra.

How do I sign up? ›

It's super easy:

  1. Go to the FindMySize homepage
  2. Fill out the form with your shoe details (brand, size, etc.)
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Click "Notify Me When Available"

That's it! We'll start monitoring for your shoe immediately. The whole process takes less than 60 seconds.

Can I request multiple shoes? ›

Yes! You can submit as many alert requests as you want. Each submission takes about 30 seconds.

This is perfect for:

  • Shopping for the whole family
  • Requesting the same shoe in multiple colors
  • Tracking different brands/models
  • Monitoring both regular and sale prices

Just fill out the form again for each shoe you want to track.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Sportscene
  • Totalsports
  • The Athletes Foot
  • Cape Union Mart
  • Edgars
  • And more...

We're constantly adding new retailers. Have a suggestion? Contact us!

How fast will I be notified? ›

We check retailers regularly, but timing varies:

  • Typical notification: Within hours of a shoe becoming available
  • Popular sizes: Very fast - we prioritize high-demand sizes
  • Weekend restocks: May take slightly longer

Important: Even with fast notifications, popular sizes can sell out quickly. Act fast when you receive an alert!

What if I don't know the exact style code? ›

That's okay! Style codes are optional. You can provide:

  • Best: Style code (e.g., FD2722-001) - guarantees exact match
  • Good: Product URL from any retailer - we'll match it exactly
  • Also works: Just brand, model, color, and size - we'll do our best to match

The more specific you are, the better we can match, but we'll work with whatever info you provide!

Do you track shoe widths (wide, narrow)? ›

Yes! We have a width field on the form where you can specify:

  • Regular / Standard (default)
  • Wide
  • Extra Wide
  • Narrow

If you're not sure, select "Regular" - it fits most people. Only specify wide or narrow if you know you need it.

📧 Notifications & Emails

I didn't receive a notification. Why? ›

Several possible reasons:

  • Check spam folder - Sometimes emails get filtered
  • Shoe not restocked yet - We can only notify when it's actually available
  • Email typo - Double-check the email you provided
  • Popular size sold out fast - By the time we checked, it was gone again
  • Exact match not found - If you were vague about model/color, we might not have found the right one

Pro tip: Add our email address (findmysizealerts@gmail.com) to your contacts to ensure delivery!

Will I get spam emails? ›

No! We ONLY email you about shoes YOU specifically requested.

You will NOT receive:

  • Random product promotions
  • Shoes you didn't ask for
  • Newsletter emails
  • Marketing messages
  • Third-party advertising

If you requested 3 shoes, you'll only get notifications about those 3 shoes.

How do I unsubscribe? ›

Easy! You have two options:

  1. Reply to any notification email with "UNSUBSCRIBE" in the subject or body
  2. Contact us directly at findmysizealerts@gmail.com

We'll remove your data within 48 hours and confirm via email.

Can I update my email address? ›

Yes! Just contact us with:

  • Your old email address
  • Your new email address
  • Which shoe request(s) to update

We'll update it within 24-48 hours.

🛒 Purchasing & Pricing

Do you sell the shoes? ›

No. FindMySize is NOT a shoe store.

We're a notification service that helps you FIND shoes at other retailers. When we notify you, we provide a link to the retailer where you can purchase the shoe directly from them.

Think of us as your personal shopping assistant who watches stores for you, not the store itself.

Are the prices guaranteed? ›

No. We show you the price at the time we found the shoe, but:

  • Retailers can change prices at any time
  • Sales may have ended by the time you click
  • Prices may vary by region or shipping

Always check the retailer's website for the current price before purchasing.

What if the shoe is sold out when I click? ›

Unfortunately, this can happen. Popular sizes sell out FAST - sometimes within minutes.

Tips to improve your chances:

  • Enable mobile notifications for emails
  • Act immediately when you receive an alert
  • Keep payment info saved on retailer sites for faster checkout
  • Submit your request again - maybe it'll restock again!

We can't control how fast shoes sell, but we notify you as quickly as possible.

What about returns or shipping? ›

Returns, shipping, customer service, and all purchase-related issues are handled by the retailer, not FindMySize.

Each retailer has their own policies for:

  • Shipping costs and delivery times
  • Return windows and conditions
  • Refund processing
  • Customer support

Always check the retailer's policies before purchasing. If you have issues with an order, contact the retailer directly.

💰 How We Make Money

If it's free, how do you make money? ›

Great question! We're transparent about this:

We earn affiliate commissions. When we send you a notification, the link may be an affiliate link. If you purchase through that link, the retailer pays us a small commission (typically 3-8% of the sale).

Important: This commission does NOT increase the price you pay. The retailer pays us from their margin - you pay the exact same price as any other customer.

Does the affiliate model affect your recommendations? ›

No. We have a strict policy:

We notify you based ONLY on:

  • YOUR requested preferences (brand, size, color, etc.)
  • Actual product availability at retailers

We do NOT:

  • Prioritize retailers that pay higher commissions
  • Send you shoes you didn't request
  • Recommend products just to earn commissions

Our reputation depends on being trustworthy. If we spammed you with unwanted products, you'd unsubscribe immediately - and we'd lose your trust.

Do I have to use your affiliate links? ›

No. You're free to shop wherever you want!

Our links are just convenient shortcuts to the product we found. You can:

  • Use our link (helps support the service)
  • Search for the shoe yourself on other sites
  • Compare prices across retailers
  • Buy in-store if you prefer

We just appreciate if you use our links when convenient - it helps us keep the service free for everyone!

🔒 Privacy & Security

What data do you collect? ›

We collect only what we need to provide the service:

  • Email address - To send you notifications
  • Shoe preferences - Brand, model, size, width, color, gender
  • Optional info - Style codes, product URLs you provide
  • Timestamp - When you submitted your request

We do NOT collect:

  • Payment information (we don't handle purchases)
  • Browsing history or cookies
  • Personal identification beyond email
  • Location data (beyond what's in your IP address for security)
Is my data safe? ›

Yes. We take security seriously:

  • Data is stored securely in Google Sheets with restricted access
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Only authorized personnel can access your data
  • We comply with POPIA (South African data protection law)
  • We never sell your data to third parties

Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I request my data or have it deleted? ›

Yes! Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Fix any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Have your data completely removed
  • Portability: Get your data in a readable format

Contact us to exercise any of these rights. We'll respond within 30 days as required by law.

Do you share my email with retailers? ›

No. Absolutely not.

Your email address is private and used ONLY to send you notifications about shoes YOU requested. We never share, sell, or give your email to retailers, advertisers, or anyone else.

🔧 Troubleshooting

The form isn't working / I get an error ›

Try these steps:

  1. Check required fields - Make sure you filled in brand, size, and email
  2. Email match - Confirm your email addresses match exactly
  3. Clear browser cache - Sometimes old data causes issues
  4. Try a different browser - Chrome or Firefox work best
  5. Disable ad blockers - They can sometimes interfere

Still not working? Contact us with details about the error and we'll help!

I submitted a request but haven't heard anything in weeks ›

A few possibilities:

  • Shoe hasn't restocked - Some shoes are genuinely hard to find or discontinued
  • Check spam folder - Our emails might be getting filtered
  • Email typo - Double-check the address you provided
  • Sold out too fast - Popular sizes can sell in minutes

Want us to check if your request is in our system? Contact us with the details of what you requested.

The link in my notification doesn't work ›

This usually means the product is no longer available at that retailer. It could be:

  • Sold out (happens fast with popular sizes)
  • Removed from the website
  • Temporarily unavailable

What to do:

  • Check other retailers manually
  • Submit your request again - it might restock later
  • Contact the retailer directly to ask about availability
